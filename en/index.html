FAQ

Before you ask — and you can still ask after

I only have an idea. Is that enough?

Absolutely. Most of our projects start with the sentence “would it be possible to make…”. We help turn the idea into a buildable plan.

What does modelling and printing a single replacement part cost?

Typically from tens of euros to a few hundred, depending on the size and complexity of the part. You always get a fixed quote before anything starts.

Do you deliver across Finland?

Yes. Physical parts by post; smart home and software work can be done fully remotely. In the Tampere region we also do on-site visits.

How quickly will I get a finished part?

A straightforward replacement part is often ready within a week. Electronics and software projects are scoped case by case — we give a realistic timeline in the quote.

Do your smart home solutions require subscriptions or a cloud service?

No. We build local-first solutions on Home Assistant and ESPHome. Your devices keep working even if the internet connection drops.

Do you work with companies too?

Yes — small production runs, prototypes and technical consulting, invoiced normally. Business ID and paperwork are all in order.

Tell us what you need — even if you don't yet know how it's made

The quote is always free and doesn't commit you to anything. We usually reply the same day.
